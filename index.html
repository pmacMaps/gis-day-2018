The Anatomy of a Web Map App

At Central PA GIS Day 2018, I presented a two-part series on "The Anatomy of a Web Map App." We looked at the Esri Shortlist StoryMap as a case study.

Part 1 focused on reviewing the Shortlist app, how to deploy a web map app, and an overview of Hypertext Markup Language (HTML).

Part 2 reviewed Cascading Stylesheets (CSS) and JavaScript. We also looked at how to apply custom CSS and JavaScript to the Shortlist app.
